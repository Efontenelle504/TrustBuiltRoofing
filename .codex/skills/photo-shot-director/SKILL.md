# Photo Shot Director

Every image must build trust, clarify the offer, or show proof. Decoration is not enough.

## Workflow

1. Inventory every image slot.
2. Define why the image exists.
3. Decide if the current image feels real, local, specific, and credible.
4. Preserve placeholders only when they clearly guide the future real shot.
5. Reject fake people, fake crews, fake logos, and AI-looking trust imagery.

## Output

Return a shot list with:
- page location,
- current image or placeholder,
- purpose,
- required real-world shot,
- framing notes,
- lighting and context notes,
- trust/conversion reason,
- alt text direction,
- replacement priority.

## Rules

- Prefer real job photos, finished work, detail shots, homeowner handoff, branded sign, documented inspection, and before/after context.
- For placeholder images, make the replacement direction obvious.
- Do not remove placeholders unless the replacement is more believable or clearer.
- Do not approve images that make the business look fake.
